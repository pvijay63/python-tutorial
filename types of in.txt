inheritance is getting the properties from parent class to child class
'''
types of inheritance
 1.single inheritance
 2.multilevel inheritance
 3.multiple inheritance
 4.hierachical inheritance
 '''
 1.single inheritance;
    parent
      | 
    child
 2.multilevel inheritance
     parent1
        |    
     parent2
        |
     child
3.multiple inheritance
   father   mother
          |
        child
4.hierachical inheritance
        parent
          |
    child1 child2
